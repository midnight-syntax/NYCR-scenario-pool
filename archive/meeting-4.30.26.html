Solitude + The World

Solitude is sacred for many artists — it gives us space to hear ourselves clearly.

But creating in a vacuum can stifle creativity. Sometimes, to write our best songs or make our best work, we have to re-enter the world:

sit on a bench in the park,
wander through a museum,
watch a film with a beautiful story,
observe strangers, colors, sounds, weather, movement.

Then we return inward — fueled with real material to create from.
